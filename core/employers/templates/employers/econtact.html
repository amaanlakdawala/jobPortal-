Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: How do I post a job listing on Talent Trove?
Posting a job listing on Talent Trove is simple. First, log in to your employer account. Navigate to the 'Post a Job' section, fill out the required details such as job title, description, requirements, and location, then click 'Submit'. Your job listing will be reviewed and published on the platform, making it visible to potential candidates.
Question 2: How can I manage the applications I receive for my job postings?
To manage applications, log in to your Talent Trove employer account and go to the 'Profile' section. Here, you will find a list of all your posted jobs. Click on the name of the candidate to view the applications.
Question 3: What should I do if I encounter technical issues while using Talent Trove?
If you experience any technical issues while using Talent Trove, please contact our support team for assistance. You can reach out via the 'Contact Us' section in your Menu, or email us at talenttrove@gmail.com. Provide a detailed description of the issue, and our team will respond promptly to help resolve it.
